The Mane Frame Radio

Mane Frame Radio is an internet radio station founded by ShadowPony in January of 2015. Created from an old Minecraft server he had laying around, the server now hosts a wide assortment of music from the Brony fandom. The station's mascot is Silver Leaf, a Pegasus that enjoys being outdoors surrounded by the elements when she's not plugged into the Mane Frame. More than a year after its creation, MFR is still committed to providing listeners with a variety of music and being involved in the community, working hard to constantly improve and better provide for our fans.
